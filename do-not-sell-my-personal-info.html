Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Last updated: October 20, 2025

Belá Beverage Co respects your privacy. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information.

Belá Beverage Co does not sell personal information to third parties. However, we provide this page to honor your rights and clarify our practices.

Your Rights

  • Request disclosure of what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future sale or sharing of your data

How to Submit a Request

To exercise your rights, please email us at privacy@belabeverage.com or use our Contact Form. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. We may require verification of your identity and authorization.

Also see our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
