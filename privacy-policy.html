Privacy Policy

At Belá, we care about your privacy as much as we care about bold flavor and good vibes. When you visit our site, sign up for updates, or interact with our content, we may collect basic information—like your name, email address, or browsing behavior—to help us improve your experience and keep you in the loop about launches you’ll love.

What We Collect

  • Contact information (like your name and email)
  • Browsing data (like IP address, device type, and pages visited)
  • Information you choose to share with us (like through forms or feedback)

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand how people use our site and to make it better. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookie preferences.

How We Use Your Info

  • Send updates, offers, and flavor news (only if you’ve opted in)
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Respond to questions or feedback
  • Keep things secure and running smoothly

Sharing & Security

We don’t sell your data. We may share it with trusted third-party services (like email platforms or analytics tools) only when necessary to operate the site or deliver what you’ve requested. We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data
  • Reach out with any privacy-related questions at sip@belaspritz.com

Age Restriction

Belá is not intended for users under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 21. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Belá grows. Any changes will be posted here, and we’ll update the effective date below.
